Ps Vita

The PlayStation Vita, released in 2011, was Sony's ambitious handheld gaming console that boasted impressive graphics and a unique touch-based control system. Despite its technical prowess, the Vita faced tough competition and struggled to gain a significant foothold in the handheld gaming market, ultimately leading to its discontinuation in 2019.
